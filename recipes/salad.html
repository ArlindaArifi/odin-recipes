Bruschetta Salad

Description

This salad tastes just like bruschetta, but in a bowl. If you like bruchetta, you will love this salad.

Ingredients

  • Roma tomatoes
  • mozzarella cheese
  • garlic
  • basil leaves
  • red onion
  • olive oil
  • red wine vinegar
  • salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, bagel chips, basil, red onion, olive oil, red wine winegar, garlic, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl; toss until evenly combined. Refrigerate until chilled, 15 to 30 minutes.
